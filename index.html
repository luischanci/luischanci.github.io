Welcome


I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the State University of New York at Binghamton. I also hold an appointment as professor of economics at U.S.T.-Chile. My research interests are in Macroeconomics, Econometrics, and Industrial Organization. This semester I am teaching Introduction to Econometrics at Binghamton University and working on my thesis.
